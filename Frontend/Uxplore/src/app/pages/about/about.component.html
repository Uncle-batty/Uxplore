Background

The app is designed to serve as a comprehensive platform for both individual users looking to find local events and activities, and businesses aiming to promote their events. The app's core functionality revolves around providing a user-friendly interface where users can browse, register, and participate in events, while businesses can manage, advertise and improve on their offerings.
